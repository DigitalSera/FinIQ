# FinIQ

FinIQ is a lightweight financial intelligence tool designed to help informal retail businesses track their transactions, understand their profit, and detect hidden financial leaks.

The application transforms simple business activities into meaningful financial insights that empower small business owners to make better decisions.

Live Demo

The application is deployed online via GitHub Pages.

Live Demo:
https://digitalsera.github.io/FinIQ/

Problem

Many small retail businesses lack access to financial tracking tools. Sales are often recorded manually, making it difficult for business owners to understand their real profit, expenses, and overall business health.

This lack of financial visibility can lead to poor decisions, hidden losses, and slow business growth.

Solution

FinIQ provides a simple financial intelligence platform that enables business owners to:

Track sales and expenses

Monitor inventory levels

Analyze profit and revenue trends

Detect financial risks and profit leaks

Receive simple insights through an AI assistant

Features

Financial dashboard with real-time analytics

Point of sale system for recording transactions

Inventory tracking with low stock alerts

Expense tracking

Financial health scoring system

Profit leakage detection

AI-powered business insights

Financial glossary for learning key concepts
